# Attention Is All You Need

## Paper Information

Authors:
Vaswani et al.

Year:
2017


## Problem

Before Transformers, sequence models depended heavily on RNNs and CNNs.


## Proposed Method

The paper introduced the Transformer architecture based entirely on attention mechanisms.


## Key Contribution

- Self Attention
- Multi Head Attention
- Positional Encoding


## Impact

The Transformer became the foundation of:

- GPT
- BERT
- LLaMA
- Modern LLMs
